Introduction

Welcome to my guide on how to create a website using Angular! This guide is designed for individuals who have basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and are looking to expand their skills by diving into the world of Angular.

did you know Angular is a popular frontend framework developed by Google?

In this guide, I will walk you through the process of setting up your development environment, installing Angular and Tailwindcss and building a web application.

Prerequisites

  • HTML: You should be familiar with the basic structure of a webpage.
  • CSS: A basic understanding of styling elements.
  • JavaScript: Basic knowledge of JavaScript, including variables, functions, loops and events.
